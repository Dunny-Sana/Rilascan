Welcome to RilaScan

Established in 2013, Rilascan Accountants provides accounting and tax services including preparation of annual financial statement services, registration, payroll and SARS E-filing for companies. We’re registered with the South African Institute of Charted Accountants, South African Institute of Business Accountant and registered Tax practitioners with South African institute of Tax professionals. Our fees and cost structure are very competitive.
